About TracerBody

We are a team of cybersecurity researchers dedicated to advancing security through ethical research and responsible disclosure practices.

Our Mission

To advance cybersecurity through responsible research, breach awareness, and ethical data disclosure practices that benefit the entire security community.

Ethical Research

Conducting security research with the highest ethical standards and responsible disclosure practices.

Knowledge Sharing

Sharing research findings, tools, and datasets to advance the cybersecurity community's collective knowledge.

Community Impact

Building tools and resources that help security professionals protect organizations and individuals.

Our Values

The principles that guide our research and shape our approach to cybersecurity.

Transparency

We believe in open and transparent research practices. Our methodologies, findings, and tools are shared with the community to advance collective security knowledge.

Responsibility

We conduct all research with the highest ethical standards, ensuring that our work contributes positively to cybersecurity without causing harm.

Collaboration

We actively collaborate with security researchers, organizations, and the broader cybersecurity community to share knowledge and improve security for everyone.

Innovation

We continuously push the boundaries of cybersecurity research, developing new techniques and tools to stay ahead of emerging threats.
